What is the Mirror?

It’s a reading assistant for Tobyworld’s Lore. You can ask questions in natural language. The Mirror pulls relevant scrolls and highlights key lines.

  • Use clear questions (“What does Scroll 2 say about patience?”).
  • Try keywords (e.g., virtues, lotus, reflection).
  • Follow links to read the full scrolls.

Tips & Notes

  • Results are grounded in our stored scrolls; we update them over time.
  • Language: use your browser’s translate feature if needed.
  • Respect the Lore. Cite what you share and don’t misrepresent it.
