How to Contribute

We’re currently invite-only. You can still prepare your piece following the rules below and contact us — we’ll add it manually.

Contact

Send us a DM with your files and metadata:

DM us on X Telegram Email

Replace links with your real handles/emails when you deploy.

What to Send

  • Artwork file (image JPG/PNG ≤ 5 MB; video MP4 H.264 ≤ 20 MB; gif ≤ 8 MB)
  • Poster image for videos (recommended)
  • Metadata (see JSON example)
  • Date your work was created/published (YYYY-MM-DD)

Filenames & Folders

  • Images → /contributors/assets/images/
  • Videos → /contributors/assets/videos/
  • GIFs → /contributors/assets/gifs/
  • Use lowercase, hyphenated names (e.g., lotus-over-the-pond.jpg)

JSON Metadata Example

{
  "artist": "@YourHandle",
  "artist_type": "X handle",
  "title": "Lotus Over the Pond",
  "media_type": "image",
  "file": "/contributors/assets/images/lotus-over-the-pond.jpg",
  "date": "2025-08-03",
  "description": "In the still water, Toby meets its reflection — the Mirror holding light and shadow."
}

For videos, include "poster": "/contributors/assets/images/lotus-poster.jpg" and set "media_type": "video".

Review & Publishing

  • We check for quality, format, author attribution, and fit with the Lore.
  • Approved works are added to the gallery (usually within a few days).
  • By submitting, you allow us to display your work on ToadAid with credit.
