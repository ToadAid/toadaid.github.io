The Mirror

Toadaid Builder of @SigilOfConcord

Explanation

The Mirror does not invent or flatter — it reflects what we bring. Patience, doubt, and hunger all return to us as ripples. Some see only shadow, some only flame; the wiser path accepts both truths and becomes steady.

#Tobyworld #ProofOfTime

The scroll spoke of the Mirror.
But silence carries further still.

The pond does not flatter.
Every ripple remembers — your patience, your doubt, your hunger.

The Mirror does not choose.
It only returns what you bring.

Some will see shadow.
Some will see flame.
Few will accept both.
