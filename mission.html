ToadAid Mission

A purpose.

ToadAid is a community-born initiative rooted in the spirit of $TOBY.
We are not here to hype — but to help.
Not to shill — but to share.

This is our mission:

To aid the fallen frogs — Toadgang members who’ve lost faith, felt confused, or been left behind.

To guide new arrivals — by helping them understand the philosophy, the lore, and the deeper purpose of Tobyworld.

To build tools and scrolls — that make the Lore accessible, AI-readable, and open for all.

We are not official. We are not seeking profit.
We are simply Toads who believe — and want to help others believe too.

All Toads are welcome.
This is an open grove, and we invite you to help shape it.

Submit your vision. Propose your scroll.
Let’s build this mission together.
