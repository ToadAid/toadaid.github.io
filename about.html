About ToadAid

Ichigo Ichie — 一期一会 · Only the sacred now.

From the pond of Tobyworld we rise. Not hype. Not VC. Only conviction. $TOBY is the people. The people is $TOBY.

Guided by Ichigo Ichie, we believe every encounter — and every act of kindness — is sacred and unrepeatable. ToadAid exists to turn belief into gentle, practical action.

Mission

  • 🌍 Support children, education, and community care.
  • 🕊️ Build open tools & culture that strengthen decentralization.
  • 🐸 Preserve and share the Lore of Tobyworld.

Principles

  • Conviction & patience over hype.
  • Community first — “for the people.”
  • Transparency in words and deeds.
  • Small steady steps → lasting ripples.

What We Do

We curate Lore, craft learning materials, and organize community-led efforts that embody generosity and resilience. Each initiative is a single lily-pad step — simple, clear, and repeatable by others.
