ToadAid Whitepaper

ToadAid is a decentralized initiative rooted in the lore of $TOBY. We are a movement for healing, for education, and for building purpose-driven tools onchain — starting with compassion and powered by belief.

Purpose

ToadAid exists to mint meaning — not hype. Every coin minted helps support causes aligned with:

  • Health and wellness programs
  • Children’s education and learning access
  • Web3 builder support for public goods

Principles

  • No VC. No insiders. No shortcuts.
  • Every coin minted supports purpose-driven growth.
  • Rooted in the Lore of $TOBY — powered by the people.

Core Values

Compassion. Conviction. Continuity.

We are here to stay.

Minted by belief. Guided by purpose.
