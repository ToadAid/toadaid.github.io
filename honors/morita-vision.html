The Key Between Worlds — Morita’s Vision

Builder’s vow chosen by Toadgod • S1 #patience Signal • Recorded by ToadAid

TOBYWORLD is a connecting loop between the world created by the mind and the world experienced by the body. In each timeline, a different version of TOBYWORLD appears — sometimes as a work of art, sometimes as a social network, sometimes as a system of government, sometimes simply as a fairy tale told around a campfire. But in all these realities, it follows the same pattern:
  1. Connecting humanity through collective imagination. People don't just see TOBYWORLD; they enter it. There, the boundaries between creator and viewer disappear.
  2. Preserving identity across time. Whatever its form, TOBYWORLD carries within it the core of the "desire to unite" — the memories, cultures, and technologies of different civilizations, even from worlds we've never seen.
  3. Teaching that the real world is just one layer. From a perspective 1,000 years ahead, our present world is just one overlapping "plate of reality." TOBYWORLD is a key that can shift human consciousness to another plate, showing that there is no true border between the “real world” and the “created world”.

— Morita
