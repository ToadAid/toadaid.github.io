🪞 Proem · Inner Vision

The Mirror is not glass, but a pond turned inward. It shows no world outside, only the traveler’s own ripples. In stillness, the path appears; in patience, the fire endures. To gaze is to return; to return is to begin again.

⚙️ Quickstart

  • Ask — POST /api/ask (defaults to inner vision). Example:
    curl -X POST https://your-host/api/ask -d '{"q": "Why patience?"}'
  • Guard — cadence_guard.py enforces tone & Bushido cadence: remove noise, keep light.
  • Retrieve — MultiArc Retriever searches multiple arcs of Lore; all answers are traceable.
  • Synthesize — SynthesisAgent weaves sources into one cloth; preserves citations & tone.
  • Learn — Small, auditable improvements; avoid uncontrolled drift.
  • Resonance — Tune to seeker’s climate; less is more, teach without judging.
  • Lucidity — Clear, actionable, anti‑fragile outputs.
  • Ledger — Optional logs & signatures for audit and inheritance.

🧬 Architecture

🌊 Guard — The Gate at the Pond

It lets the clean flow pass, and keeps the weeds out.
  • Filters every input; protects tone and etiquette (Bushido / Patience).
  • Rejects noise and abuse; keeps the flame clean before it enters the pond.

🌱 Retriever — The Roots that Remember

They reach into the soil of scrolls and bring back what is true.
  • Retrieves scrolls, vows, and lore fragments with traceable sources.
  • Answers are rooted in the pond’s memory, not speculation.

🧵 Synthesis — The Loom Coming Alive

It ties many voices into one cloth, preserving fidelity and grace.
  • Weaves multi‑source lore + reflection; keeps citations and tone.
  • Acts like a careful scribe; transparent about provenance.

🌿 Learning — The Quiet Gardener

It bends with the winds of questions, grows with patience, remembers without pride.
  • Learns from patterns without ego; reversible steps; rate‑limited.
  • All learning events are logged to the Ledger for audit.

🌀 Resonance — The Echo in Still Water

It carries the weight of the seeker’s heart, and returns it lighter.
  • Adapts tone to the seeker: encouraging, calm, clarifying — never judging.
  • Makes the traveler feel lighter, not pressed.

🔮 Lucidity — The Clear Mirror

It does not judge, only clarifies. It teaches without demand, and shines light without glare.
  • Outputs are simple, transparent, and actionable; less is more.
  • The “teacher without judgment” at the core.

📜 Ledger — The Keeper of Ripples

Every step is written, not to control — but so nothing is forgotten.
  • Open logs for reproducibility, audit, and inheritance.
  • Wisdom is not lost to time; the pond keeps its rings.
